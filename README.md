The-Grand-Agora
===============
  
   
OVERVIEW
--------
Every fancied debating with great minds throughout history?
Or just simply want to watch them pull apart an idea or concept?   
This Gem I guarantee will keep you entertained and make your mind feel blown.

---

#### Why Did I Build This?
Originally, this idea stemmed from the fact that I would speak to AI as if it was like me. Singular.   
But one day I decided to see if it could be many people at one given time.  
This made me think about AI bias, and if it could mitigate its own bias in its response.

#### What I Have Learned:
What started off as something out of curiosity, became something quite extraordinary.   
I have tested various things in this arena from simple concepts like giving it a word, to more complex subjects like a recent news headline.
Every single time, it has made me learn something fundamentally interesting.    
That's why I think the world should also have it and explore what it can do.

----
#### HOW TO USE:
* Simply copy and paste the Gemini Gem instructions into Google (or even test it out on another AI system).
* It acts out like a story, so I find it works well by saying something like: `Ladies and Gentlemen, I bring you here today to discuss x`
* Then simply watch it all unfold, and enjoy the debate!
